What is TECHO?

It is a non-profit organization that, with the support of young volunteers, fights extreme poverty in Latin America through housing construction and social programs.

Participation:

During this volunteer trip, I had the opportunity to participate in the construction of a house by laying piles.

Learning:

I had the opportunity to learn more about the house-building methods they use, as well as connect with people firsthand.

What is Prep School?

It is a Superate program that provides supplementary education to seventh and eighth grade students in different subjects, allowing them to develop new skills and learn about the program's scholarship.

Participation:

I participated in this project as an intern with the Superate program, primarily assisting with administrative tasks and communications with parents.

Leaning:

I learned about organizing classes and the administrative logistics involved in handling the personal data of students.
