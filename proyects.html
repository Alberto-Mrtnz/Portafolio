Proyects

Main technological works that have been done in recent years and have generated new learning.

Hackathon

SV AI-Healt

Hackathon was a project developed in the ¡Supérate! program with the aim of providing an introduction to the process of developing a digital project in teams.

App

It was SV AI-Health, an application that allows users to detect allergenic ingredients in each product they scan. They can also access a list of products with specifications of their ingredients and possible allergens they may cause.

Development

The project was carried out through Power Apps, where all its functionalities, such as the database, were built using other Microsoft Office apps.

Role

App development

Duration

2 months

Expo de logros

Eventi

Expo de Logros was a project in which a digital solution to a problem was developed. During this process, surveys and interviews were conducted to investigate the problem further, then a website was developed to provide a solution to the problem, and finally it was presented at an expo with different projects.

App

Eventi, a website where users can explore all kinds of services related to any celebration or event, and can also plan their celebration through boards that allow them to keep every element of the celebration organized.

Development

Using programming languages such as PHP, Laravel, JavaScript, and some libraries and frameworks such as Tailwind CSS and Alpine JS. The database was created in MySQL.

Role

UX/UI Designer and Front-end developer

Duration

3 months

Expo-Tech

TrackMate

Expo Tech was a project that focused on developing technological projects with the potential to monetize and respond to real problems, which were then presented at a Shark Tank event with judges from multiple national and international technology companies.

App

Track Mate, an app that connects vehicle owners with mechanics, providing benefits to both parties. It allows users to keep track of their vehicles and easily contact their preferred mechanic, while mechanics can manage their workshops.

Development

The first phase consisted of presenting the visual elements of the brand and the programming part of the project. The second phase consisted of giving a general presentation on the entire project in a Shark Tank format.

Role

Front-end developer

Duration

1 month
